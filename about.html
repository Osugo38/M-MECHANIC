ABOUT

The M-Mechanic Ltd. was established in july 2019 as a problem-solver to both mechanics and drivers. this web application is to be used by drivers in times when drivers get mechanical problems when they are travelling to areas unfamiliar to them. For this reason M-Mechanics came in to help stuck drivers by providing them with contacts of sorrounding mechanics or towing services where they can get their cars fixed or towed to a better or safer place convinient for them.The need for such an application led to our partnering with different global providers such as the uber,safaricom,taxify,bolt etc to facilitate assistance of the same.
